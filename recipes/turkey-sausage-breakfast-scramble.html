Turkey Sausage Breakfast Scramble

Description

This hearty breakfast scramble is loaded with fluffy eggs, sautéed spinach, juicy tomatoes, and protein-packed turkey sausage. It’s a nutritious and delicious way to start your day, keeping you satisfied for hours.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cooked turkey sausage, chopped
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 slice whole-grain toast
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the spinach and cook until wilted, about 2 minutes.
  3. Add the diced tomato and turkey sausage, cooking for another 2 minutes.
  4. Whisk the eggs in a bowl, then pour them into the skillet.
  5. Stir gently to scramble the eggs with the sausage and vegetables.
  6. Cook until the eggs are fully set, about 3-4 minutes.
  7. Serve with whole-grain toast and avocado slices on the side.
