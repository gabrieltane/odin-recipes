Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry

Description

This colorful and vibrant stir-fry combines tender chicken pieces with crisp bell peppers, broccoli, and snow peas, all tossed in a savory soy and garlic sauce. It's a quick and easy dinner option that’s both nutritious and packed with protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup snow peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the bell pepper, broccoli, and snow peas, and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and stir to coat the ingredients evenly.
  6. Cook for an additional 2 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  7. Serve with steamed rice and garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
